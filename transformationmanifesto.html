Manifesto for successful organisational transformation

The agile manifesto for software delivery falls short of delivering a structure for organisational change.
We have defined a maniesto for successful agile organisational transformation to resolve this issue.
With our collective experience we have created what we see as the 5 Keys to successful organisational transformation.

We value
Whole organisational transformation NOT change in silos

Starting small and growing with success NOT obsessing with scale

Grounded in reality NOT blind optimism (recognising the magnitude of the challenge)

Committed leadership NOT absentee leaders

Peoples experience, expertise and mindset (Leading with the right people) NOT those who are simply available and certified

The things on the right have no intrinsic value to supporting your transformation and are a time and energy sink.

Principles of the manifesto

These priniples define the manifesto.

Committed leadership, not absentee leaders
The best transformations arise from a clearly articulated need for change
Leaders work together to define, articulate and drive towards a common vision
Continuous leadership support, engagement and involvement throughout is crucial for a successful outcome


Whole organisational transformation not change in silos
Use technology initiatives as an opportunity to build your future organisation
Build work around cross functional teams focussed on a thing of value to minimise waste and cross pollinate knowledge
Commit to agile practice across the entire lifecycle of work, for all organisational functions


Starting small and growing with success not obsessing with scale
Prioritise work that can succeed in taking your organisation forwards into the new state
Build internal capability to ensure long-term ownership and momentum
Scale based on success, lessons learned, and available opportunities
Apply a flexible, best practice approach, understanding different agile methods and frameworks can be used in different areas of your business


Ensure your transformation is grounded in reality not blind optimism
Recognise the magnitude of the challenge and the impact it will have on ALL areas of your business
Be clear on why work is important, and how it contributes to the realisation of organisational goals
Successful transformations are supported by well thought through planning and an adaptive mindset
Generate focus on transformation through effective business prioritisation and limiting WIP


Leading with the right people not those who are simply available
Select the best people for your transformation based on motivation, mindset and skills
Successful transformation is underpinned by resilient and motivated individuals - look after them
